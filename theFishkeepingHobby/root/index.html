The Fishkeeping Hobby

Fishkeeping is the hobby that consists in creating a small habitat within an aquarium or a tank where your fish can live and thrive, and it is a hobby that can vary widely depending on each fish-keeper’s personal preferences as some keepers and aquarists like to create environments with live plants and fish species that would live together in the wild, aiming to decorate their aquariums with natural elements such as driftwood, sand, gravel and stones, while others may prefer more artificial aesthetics, mixing different species of fish from different areas of the world and using all kinds of ornaments to create aquariums that fit their personal preferences.

Regardless of the fish-keeper that you are, or if you are just entering the hobby, there are a few basic elements to the fishkeeping hobby that you can explore throughout this site, which will focus on freshwater fishkeeping.
